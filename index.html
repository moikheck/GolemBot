Welcome

We are the Eden historians. We are a group of people dedicated to finding out the history and culture of every civilization before our own. Our goals are to make sure we can study and learn from the past, and aim to never repeat the mistakes we made.

We look to continue expanding our website to include major topics from previous eras in Eden, especially those related to the Disasters, as well as the cultures surrounding them.

If you are finding this and are from a society without computers, hello! You can find more information by clicking "Eden Historians" at the top. If you are finding this fragmented and broken, click the same page.
